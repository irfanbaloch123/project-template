## About

> A full stack developer is a software engineer who has expertise in both front-end and back-end development. They are proficient in multiple programming languages, frameworks, and databases, and are capable of creating and maintaining complex web applications from end to end. Their skills allow them to work on all aspects of a web project, including design, development, testing, deployment, and maintenance.
